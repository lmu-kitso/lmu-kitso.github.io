Our lab is featured in "2036 – Gesund mit KI", an interactive public outreach project by the Munich Medicine Alliance (M1)—a collaboration between LMU, TUM, their attached university hospitals, and Helmholtz Munich—in cooperation with the Wort & Bild Verlag. Developed as part of the Wissenschaftsjahr 2026, the platform explores how AI could shape healthcare through interactive patient journeys. 

In the Adult storyline (Glioblastoma), Kristian presents our work on predicting treatment response in glioblastoma using molecular tumor data and machine learning, as well as our research on monitoring treatment-related cardiovascular side effects with wearable devices and clinical data. 

We are pleased to contribute to this initiative, which makes current AI research accessible to a broad audience.
