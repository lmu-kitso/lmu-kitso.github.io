Our lab is now part of **TransVar64e**, a collaborative project developing new methods to improve the interpretation of cancer genomes.

By integrating DNA and RNA sequencing data, the project aims to better classify uncertain genetic variants and support more informed, personalized treatment decisions for cancer patients. TransVar64e involves all six sites of the Bavarian Center for Cancer Research (BZKF).
