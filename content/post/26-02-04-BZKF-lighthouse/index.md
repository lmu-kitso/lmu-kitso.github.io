Researchers from all six BZKF sites met in Augsburg together with nearly 20 members of the Lighthouse initiative to launch the third funding period of the BZKF Lighthouse “Omics, Genomics and Liquid Biopsy."

The highly interactive and inspiring meeting focused on planning the next steps and advancing collaborative projects. The team looks forward to translating new ideas into practice and enabling innovative diagnostic and therapeutic options in cancer medicine.

We thank all members for the excellent collaboration and the Bavarian Center for Cancer Research (BZKF) for supporting the Lighthouse initiative.
